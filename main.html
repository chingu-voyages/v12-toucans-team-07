How To Use Budget Manager

Step1 : Click on + icon at bottom right of the application. "Enter your budget details" box opens.

Step2: Fill in budget details and save your budget. Your saved budget is now displayed under "This Section displays all your budgets" section. Under the displayed budget card, you will see different information like 'Budget Name', 'Budget Value' and some icons.

Step3: To add an expense, click on + icon inside budget card. Add details and save.

Step4: To View all expenses click on 'eye' shaped icon. All the expenses under a budget, will be displayed in "Click on 'eye' icon under a budget to view its expenses." section. Expenses section shows the current budget status in color, total expenses and current balance.
